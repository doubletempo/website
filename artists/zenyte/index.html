Zenyte

Infectious, club-oriented breakbeat with heavy UK influences.

Listen Friday Night Take Over: R-Newt Lefris X Zenyte About

Zenyte manages to capture the essence of the UK sound, while giving his own unique spin to it. His sound, shaped by his love for modern breakbeat, 90's rave and hyperpop, is all about creating an energetic atmosphere, as he explores unexpected blends between the various genres that inspire him.
