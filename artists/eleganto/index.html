Eleganto

Rising prodigy with the freshest sound in house right now.

Listen STMPD RCRDS Radio 039 - Eleganto About

Eleganto is bringing a breath of fresh air to the house scene. This hasn't gone unnoticed as many big artists are following his every move closely. What started of with his EP "Frightening Beasts" and festival weapon "No Stoppin" followed in winning a remix competition for Martin Garrix "Won't Let You Go" cementing his name in the scene.

Soon after followed the genre defying singles "Just Tell Me", "Turn It Up" (with Smack) and "One Last Try". These songs display his craftsmanship in composing hard-hitting, next level productions. His versatility shows as he seamlessly releases a mix of Tech house, Bass House and Future house tunes.
